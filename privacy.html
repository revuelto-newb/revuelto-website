Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

Revuelto collects necessary data to provide a functional and engaging RPG experience. This includes:

  • Discord User IDs (to save your garage, stats, and progress).
  • Discord Server IDs (to track server-specific settings and races).
  • Basic command usage data for balancing and bug-fixing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information exclusively for operating the Revuelto bot. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. Your data is used to:

  • Maintain your personal progression and car collection.
  • Provide global leaderboards.
  • Ensure smooth performance and reliability of the bot.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is stored for as long as you use the bot. If you wish to have your data completely wiped from our databases, you can join our Support Server and open a data deletion request ticket.
