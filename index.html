More Than an Economy Bot

Revuelto is designed for servers that want a deep, engaging progression system. Unlike standard economy bots where numbers just go up, Revuelto focuses on the tangible thrill of the hunt—collecting, tuning, and racing your dream cars.

Every vehicle has distinct stats, every race requires strategy, and every prestige level earns you respect in the community.
